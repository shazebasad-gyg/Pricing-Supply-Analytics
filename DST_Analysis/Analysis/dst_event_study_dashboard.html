Methodology — YoY with pre-trend adjustment (Option 2): We compare CY (current year) pre/post performance against LY (last year) pre/post for the same calendar window. The YoY-Corrected Change isolates the policy impact by subtracting the baseline seasonal shift: YoY-Corrected Δ = (PostCY − PreCY) − (PostLY − PreLY). A negative value suggests the policy change drove an incremental decline beyond normal seasonality.
